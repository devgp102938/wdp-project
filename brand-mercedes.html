ABOUT MERCEDES

Mercedes-Benz is a German multinational automotive manufacturer known for luxury cars, vans, and trucks, and is one of the world's most successful automakers. Founded in 1926, the company has a history of innovation, particularly in safety and performance, and is a global leader in the development of high-end passenger vehicles. The company is committed to future-forward innovations, including connectivity, automated driving, and alternative drives.
