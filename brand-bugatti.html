ABOUT BUGATTI

Bugatti is a French luxury automobile manufacturer founded in 1909 by Ettore Bugatti, known for producing high-end, high-performance cars. Today, Bugatti is part of Bugatti Rimac, a joint venture between the Rimac Group and Porsche AG, and continues to hand-assemble its hypercars in Molsheim, France. The brand is associated with exclusivity, speed, and luxury, with historic models like the Type 35 and modern ones like the Chiron.
